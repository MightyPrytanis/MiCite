Free Michigan citation-formatting utility

Review Michigan legal citations before filing.

MiCite checks citation form under Michigan-style conventions, including common reporter, statute, court-rule, evidence-rule, and case-name formatting issues. It is a formatting aid, not legal research or a substitute for professional review.

Run the web app

Open MiCite in this browser. Core citation-formatting review runs locally in the browser, with no account, database, or document-upload server.

Install or download locally

Add MiCite to your device from the browser, or download a self-contained offline copy. No terminal, command line, account, or installer required.

MiCite checks citation format only. It does not validate the existence of a cited case or source, verify quotations or legal inferences, or determine whether an authority has been overruled, superseded, or otherwise limited. MiCite is a formatting tool, not a cite-checking or legal-research tool.
